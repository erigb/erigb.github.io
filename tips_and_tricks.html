When going on long hiking trips, I suggest wearing shoes that have ankle support.
This will help prevent injuries like getting a sprained ankle while walking!

When on a portage, it is very important to unpack all of your packs from the boat and place them neatly on land.
This makes sure you have a nice walk and will not forget any of your gear.
Stay organized!
